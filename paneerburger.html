Recipe: Paneer Burger

Prep time: 15 min

Cook time: 15 min

Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer (cut into thick round slices or squares, about burger patty size)
  • 2 tbsp cornflour
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • Spices: chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, salt
  • Water (for batter)
  • Bread crumbs
  • Oil (for frying)
  • 2 burger buns
  • Lettuce, onion, tomato slices
  • Cheese slices (optional)
  • Mayo & ketchup
  • Butter (for toasting)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix cornflour, maida, chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, and salt.
  2. Add water slowly to make a medium-thick batter (not too runny).
  3. Dip each paneer slice into the batter, coat it well.
  4. Roll in breadcrumbs evenly for a nice crispy crust.
  5. Heat oil in a pan. Shallow or deep fry the paneer until golden and crispy.
  6. Drain on paper towels.
  7. Slice burger buns in half and toast them on a pan with a little butter until golden.
  8. Spread mayo or desired sauce on both bun halves.
  9. Layer lettuce, crispy paneer patty, cheese slice (optional), tomato slices, onion rings.
  10. Drizzle some ketchup or extra mayo on top.
  11. Cover with the top bun.
  12. Serve hot with fries or chips and your favorite drink.
