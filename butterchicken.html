Recipe: Butter Chicken

Prep time: 15 min

Cook time: 45 min

Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 500g boneless chicken (preferably thigh), cut into pieces
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2 medium tomatoes, pureed
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 cup fresh cream
  • 1 tsp kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions

  1. Marinate the chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, and salt. Let it rest for at least 30 minutes (preferably 1 hour).
  2. Heat butter in a pan. Add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  3. Add tomato puree, turmeric, coriander powder, and cook until the oil starts separating.
  4. Add marinated chicken and cook on medium heat until the chicken is fully cooked.
  5. Stir in cream, garam masala, and crushed kasuri methi. Let it simmer for 5‚Äì10 minutes.
  6. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with naan or rice.
