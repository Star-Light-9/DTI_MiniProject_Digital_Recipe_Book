Recipe: Sushi

Prep time: 55 min

Cook time: 20 min

Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sushi rice
  • 2 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 4 sheets nori (seaweed)
  • 1 small cucumber, julienned
  • 1 small carrot, julienned
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 100g cooked or raw fish (like salmon or tuna) ‚Äì optional
  • Soy sauce, for serving
  • Pickled ginger & wasabi (optional)
  • Bamboo sushi mat (for rolling)

Instructions

  1. Rinse sushi rice thoroughly. Cook according to package instructions.
  2. In a small bowl, mix rice vinegar, sugar, and salt. Heat slightly to dissolve, then mix into the cooked rice. Let cool.
  3. Place a sheet of nori on the bamboo mat, shiny side down.
  4. Spread a thin layer of sushi rice on the nori, leaving 1 inch of space at the top.
  5. Arrange fillings (vegetables, fish, etc.) horizontally about 1 inch from the bottom.
  6. Use the mat to carefully roll the sushi from the bottom, pressing gently to form a tight roll.
  7. Seal the edge with a little water and slice the roll into 6‚Äì8 pieces using a sharp, wet knife.
  8. Repeat for remaining ingredients.
  9. Serve with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger. Enjoy!
