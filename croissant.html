Recipe: Croissant

Prep time: 2 hours (plus chilling & proofing)

Cook time: 20 min

Servings: 8

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1/4 cup warm water
  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 1 tbsp melted butter
  • 1 cup (225g) cold unsalted butter (for laminating)
  • 1 egg (for egg wash)

Instructions

  1. Dissolve yeast in warm water and let it sit for 5â€“10 minutes until foamy.
  2. In a large bowl, mix flour, sugar, and salt. Add warm milk, melted butter, and yeast mixture. Knead into a soft dough.
  3. Cover and let rise for 1 hour or until doubled. Chill dough for 30 minutes.
  4. Roll out the dough into a rectangle. Place cold butter slab in the center and fold dough over it. Roll and fold 3 times, chilling in between each fold.
  5. Roll out final dough and cut into triangles. Roll each triangle from base to tip to form croissant shapes.
  6. Place on baking tray, cover, and let rise for 1â€“2 hours until puffy.
  7. Brush with egg wash. Bake at 200Â°C (400Â°F) for 15â€“20 minutes until golden and flaky.
  8. Cool slightly and enjoy fresh!
