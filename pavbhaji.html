Recipe: Pav Bhaji

Prep time: 15 min

Cook time: 25 min

Servings: 3

  • Green peas (boiled)
  • Onion, tomato, capsicum (chopped)
  • Ginger-garlic paste

  • Butter & oil
  • Water (for consistency)
