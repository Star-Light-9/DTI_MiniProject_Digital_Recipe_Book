Recipe: French Fries

Prep time: 15 min

Cook time: 15 min

Servings: 3

Ingredients

  • 4 large potatoes
  • Oil for deep frying
  • Salt to taste
  • Optional: paprika, garlic powder, or black pepper for seasoning

Instructions

  1. Peel and cut the potatoes into thin strips. Rinse under cold water to remove excess starch.
  2. Soak the potato strips in cold water for 30 minutes. Drain and pat them completely dry with a towel.
  3. Heat oil in a deep pan. Fry the potatoes in batches on medium heat for 5‚Äì6 minutes until soft but not golden. Remove and drain.
  4. Let them cool for a few minutes. Increase the oil temperature and fry again until golden and crispy.
  5. Drain on paper towels. Sprinkle with salt and any additional seasoning you like.
  6. Serve hot with ketchup or your favorite dip!
