Recipe: Egg Roll

Prep time: 10 min

Cook time: 15 min

Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 paratha or tortilla wraps
  • 1 small onion (finely sliced)
  • 1 small tomato (chopped)
  • 1 green chili (optional, finely chopped)
  • 1 tbsp oil or butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Tomato ketchup or green chutney (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk the eggs with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and pour the egg mixture into it, swirling slightly to spread.
  3. Place a paratha/tortilla directly over the egg while it's still wet. Press gently and cook until the egg is set and the wrap sticks.
  4. Flip and cook for another 1‚Äì2 minutes. Remove from heat.
  5. Add chopped onions, tomatoes, chilies, and sauces if using on the egg side.
  6. Roll it up tightly and cut in half if desired. Serve hot!
