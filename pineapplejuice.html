Recipe: Pineapple Juice

Prep time: 10 min

Cook time: 0 min

Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe pineapple (peeled, cored, and chopped)
  • 1‚Äì1.5 cups cold water (adjust based on desired consistency)
  • 1‚Äì2 teaspoons sugar or honey (optional, depending on sweetness of the pineapple)
  • ¬Ω teaspoon lemon or lime juice (optional, enhances flavor)
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Instructions

  1. Peel the pineapple, remove the core, and cut it into small chunks.
  2. Add the pineapple chunks to a blender along with cold water.
  3. Blend until smooth.
  4. If you prefer pulp-free juice, strain the mixture using a fine mesh sieve or cheesecloth.
  5. Taste the juice. If needed, add sugar or honey and a splash of lemon or lime juice. Blend briefly to mix.
  6. Pour into glasses over ice (optional) and serve chilled!
