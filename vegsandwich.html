Recipe: Veg Sandwich

Prep time: 10 min

Cook time: 0 min

Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 6 slices of bread (white, whole wheat, or multigrain)
  • 1 small cucumber, thinly sliced
  • 1 small tomato, thinly sliced
  • 1 small onion, thinly sliced (optional)
  • 1 small boiled potato, thinly sliced (optional)
  • Butter or margarine, as needed
  • Green chutney or mayonnaise, as needed
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • Chaat masala (optional, for extra flavor)

Instructions

  1. Trim the edges of the bread slices (optional).
  2. Spread butter evenly on one side of each slice.
  3. Spread green chutney or mayonnaise over the buttered side.
  4. Layer cucumber, tomato, onion, and potato slices on three bread slices.
  5. Sprinkle with salt, pepper, and chaat masala if using.
  6. Top with the remaining bread slices to form sandwiches.
  7. Cut diagonally or into halves and serve fresh, or toast/grill if desired.
